Hello humans, after coming to the realization that their engineering degrees aren't going to take them too far in life, three extremely unemployable students with the combined IQ of a coconut decided to start a podcast. Remember to like and share our content with your friends if you were entertained and don't forget to reach out to us for positive feedback or constructive criticism on Instagram @thelastminutepodcast.
